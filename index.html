About Me

I’m a graphic design student who deeply values thoughtful and intentional design. I’m drawn to clarity, balance, and the quiet power of details—especially in typography, layout, and visual rhythm. I enjoy creating work that feels calm, meaningful, and human, where every element has a purpose. For me, design is not only about aesthetics, but about emotion, connection, and the experience it creates.
